Histoire de ma vie...

Urvesh Ramjibhai Kabariya belongs from farmers’ family in India whose father took the lead in the family with a vision to change the family economic background and became an automobile engineer. Following on his father’s step stones and his passion, Urvesh entered the education world of mechanical-automotive engineering. He possesses Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering from California State University, Fullerton, where he contributed as a Graduate Research Assistance.

Prior to this, he served as design & services engineer in Universal Autolink, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, where he led a team of automobile engineers to develop duel fuel systems for four-wheeler automobiles and few additional projects related to alternative fuel systems. Intervening time of this services, he also completed his Post-Graduation in Marketing & Sales Management in a collaborative program compiled by California State University, San Bernardino & Ahmedabad Management Association in Gujarat, India to analyze customer requirements & expectations to improve product designs in mechanical-automotive engineering. He was used teaching students of diploma in automobile engineering the courses such as automobile component design, automobile engines, and fuels & lubricants. He completed his Bachelor’s & Diploma in Automobile Engineering from Gujarat Technological University, India. He did his entire schooling in A-One School, Memnagar, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India.

He has been awarded by non-resident tuition fee waiver award, three gold medals, a student of the year award, and an excellence award during this journey.

He loves to make short films, play cricket & yahtzee board games, and meet people during his leisure time.
